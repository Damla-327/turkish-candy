THE STORY:

During the roasting process, it is believed in ancient Turkish traditions that the scent that arises goes to the soul of the deceased. The aroma nourishes the soul of the deceased, while the helva itself feeds those left behind. The prepared semolina helva is cooked with wishes such as may the deceased find pleasure in it, and may the torment of the grave be alleviated according to Islamic belief, and it is served to guests. The month of Muharram, according to the Hijri calendar, corresponds to the time when Prophet Muhammad migrated from Mecca to Medina along with his companions to preserve their beliefs. The day of Ashura falls on the 10th day of Muharram, which is Friday, July 28, 2023. From the 10th day of Muharram onward, Ashura can be made and distributed throughout the month."
