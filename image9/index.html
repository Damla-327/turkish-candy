THE STORY:

The story of pişmaniye is about a vendor who falls in love with a woman and creates a sweet for her called şişmaniye, which means "my fat lady" in Turkish. The vendor sends the woman a few boxes of the sweet in honor of her, and they eventually marry. However, the woman becomes jealous and tricks the vendor, and he leaves. He then changes the name of the sweet to pişmaniye, which means "regret".
