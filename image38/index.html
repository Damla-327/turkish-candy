TEXTURE:

İrmik tatlisi, also known as Turkish semolina pudding or sütlü irmik tatlisi, has a soft and creamy texture. The pudding is made from cooked semolina, which gives it a soft and creamy texture. To achieve a nice texture, you can whisk the mixture while heating it, then use a spoon to stir. You can also use silicone ice cube molds to create smooth and shiny ball
